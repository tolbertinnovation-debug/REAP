Who we are

Turning resilience into opportunity for Liberia’s young people

REAP is a non-governmental, non-profit organization headquartered in Bentol City, Montserrado County, Liberia. Since 2003 we have provided the tools, educational resources and practical training that restore hope to children, orphans and young adults impacted by the nation’s civil wars.

  • Empower & equip

    We help young people become skilled, entrepreneurial and self-sustaining technicians and leaders.

  • Whole-person support

    Education is paired with psycho-social care so healing and learning happen together.

  • Rooted in community

    Graduates return value to their families, communities and the wider society.

What we do

Programs that build lasting independence

Each REAP program tackles a different barrier young Liberians face — from skills and schooling to enterprise and well-being — so that progress is durable, not temporary.

Flagship Program

REAP Vocational Training Center Institute (VTCI)

Hands-on, certified training in market-ready trades that give graduates a clear path to employment and entrepreneurship.

  • Technical & vocational skills with practical workshops
  • Entrepreneurship and small-business readiness
  • Mentorship from experienced instructors

Our approach

From vulnerability to a self-sustaining future

We meet young people where they are and walk with them until they can stand on their own — and then lift others.

Reach

Restore hope & safety

We welcome children and young adults affected by conflict, providing care, stability and psycho-social support.

Equip

Educate & train

Through schooling and vocational training, we build the knowledge and trade skills that open real opportunity.

Release

Empower & multiply

Graduates become self-sustaining technicians, entrepreneurs and leaders who strengthen their communities.
