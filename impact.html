By the numbers & beyond

What lasting impact looks like

Our goal isn’t temporary relief — it’s permanent transformation. Here’s how that shows up in real lives and communities.

Skilled, employable graduates

Young people leave the VTC Institute with a trade in hand — ready to work, build businesses and provide for their families.

Children educated & cared for

From early childhood onward, children receive schooling, stability and the psycho-social support to heal and grow.

Stronger communities

Graduates reinvest their skills locally, multiplying the impact of every life REAP equips.

A story of transformation

From the margins to a meaningful livelihood

Behind every statistic is a young person whose story changed direction at REAP.

A child who arrived without parents or hope finds safety, schooling and care. Years later, equipped with a certified trade and the confidence to lead, they step into the workforce — not as a recipient of aid, but as a provider, employer and example for others.

Multiply that journey across two decades, and you begin to see the quiet revolution REAP is building in Liberia: one restored life at a time.
