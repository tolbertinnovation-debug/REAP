REAP Vocational Training Center Institute (VTCI)

The heart of REAP’s work: hands-on, certified training that gives young people a marketable trade and a real path to employment or enterprise.

At the VTC Institute, students learn by doing. Practical workshops, experienced instructors and a focus on entrepreneurship help graduates move from the classroom to a livelihood — becoming the self-sustaining technicians their communities need.

  • Skilled trades

    Practical, market-relevant vocational and technical skills.

  • Entrepreneurship

    Small-business and self-employment readiness.

  • Certification & mentorship

    Recognized training with guidance from skilled instructors.

Early childhood

VAT & Early Childhood Development (ECDC)

A strong start changes everything. Our early childhood program nurtures the youngest children with foundational learning and dependable care.

Through early literacy, numeracy and social development in safe, supportive environments, ECDC prepares children to thrive in school and life — while engaging families and caregivers as partners.

  • Foundational learning

    Early literacy, numeracy and social-emotional growth.

  • Safe & nurturing care

    Stable, supportive spaces for young children to flourish.

  • Family engagement

    Caregivers supported as their child’s first teachers.

Leadership development

PLI Seminars & Leadership Training

Skills open doors — character keeps them open. Our PLI seminars build the leadership, values and life skills young people need to lead well.

Through practical seminars and personal development, participants grow in integrity, responsibility and vision, preparing them to serve their families and communities as ethical leaders.

  • Ethical leadership

    Character, values and integrity at the core.

  • Personal development

    Goal-setting, communication and life skills.

  • Civic responsibility

    Service and contribution to the wider community.

Long-term resilience

Sustainability Initiatives

True empowerment lasts. Our sustainability work builds self-reliance for the organization and the families we serve through agriculture and income-generating projects.

From food-security and farming projects to skills-based enterprise and environmental stewardship, these initiatives reduce dependence and create durable sources of livelihood and learning.

  • Agriculture & food security

    Farming projects that feed and fund the mission.

  • Income generation

    Enterprise that sustains programs and creates jobs.

  • Environmental stewardship

    Caring for the land that sustains the community.
